### Historical Volatility Acceleration
The German power market has recorded a structural shift away from baseload stability:
* **2024 Actuals:** 457 negative hours. Average spread: 111.20 EUR/MWh.
* **2025 Actuals:** 576 negative hours. Average spread: 124.13 EUR/MWh.
* **YoY Impact:** A 26% growth in negative hours, and an 11.6% widening of the daily spread.

### Q2 2026 Breakout
The market polarization accelerated violently in early 2026:
* **April Surge:** Negative hours exploded by +64.0% in April 2026 compared to the same month in 2025.
* **The Abregelung Floor:** On May 1st, 2026, severe solar overproduction triggered an all-time low price of -499 EUR/MWh.
